Our Vision & Approach

At Bhavini Foundation, our vision is to create a community where mental health is a top priority. We take a **holistic approach**, addressing not only the symptoms of mental illness but also the underlying causes like domestic violence, dowry issues, and sexual abuse.

"We believe in treating the person, not just the problem, by looking at biological, psychological, and social factors."

Advocacy

Standing as a beacon of support for victims of human trafficking and dowry-related concerns.

Accessibility

Expanding services and increasing accessibility to mental health resources across rural and urban communities.

Our Core Causes

Mental Health

Providing professional counseling and psychological support for victims of trauma, anxiety, and depression caused by social abuse.

Social Justice

Fighting against domestic violence, dowry demands, and sexual harassment through legal aid and awareness workshops.

Life Protection

Active intervention and rehabilitation programs for survivors of human trafficking and child exploitation.
